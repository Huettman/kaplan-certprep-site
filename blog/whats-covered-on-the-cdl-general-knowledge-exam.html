Whats Covered on the CDL General Knowledge Exam

Oct 25, 2025 · 7 min read

Introduction

The CDL General Knowledge exam is a critical first step for operating commercial motor vehicles professionally.

Vehicle Inspection

Covers pre-trip procedures, identifying mechanical issues, knowing when a vehicle is unsafe, and checking fluids, tires, brakes, and lights.

Basic Vehicle Control

Controlling speed and steering, using mirrors and signals, shifting gears and handling turns, operating in different conditions.

Safe Driving Practices

Managing space, adjusting to weather, recognizing hazards, and handling emergencies like tire blowouts or brake failure.

Transporting Cargo

Inspecting and securing cargo, understanding weight distribution and load limits, using tie-downs, preventing cargo shifts.

Rules and Regulations

Hours of Service, medical and licensing requirements, vehicle size and weight restrictions, alcohol and drug testing policies.

Preparation Tips

Create a realistic study schedule. Use official manuals and practice tests. Focus on understanding concepts. Use flexible online prep courses.
