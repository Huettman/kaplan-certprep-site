How to Pass the SHRM CP Exam on Your First Try

Aug 20, 2025 · 8 min read

Introduction

For HR professionals, the SHRM-CP credential is a key milestone. Passing on your first try requires focused preparation, time management, and understanding the exam structure.

Exam Format

160 questions: 95 knowledge items and 65 situational judgment items. 4-hour time limit. Content divided into Behavioral Competencies and HR Knowledge Domains from the SHRM BASK.

Create a Study Plan

Allow 8 to 12 weeks. Block out distraction-free sessions. Alternate reading, practice questions, and review. Include practice tests every 2 to 3 weeks.

Quality Prep Resources

Look for tools aligned with the SHRM BASK. Online courses, practice exams with explanations, flashcards, and study guides.

Situational Judgment

Practice with realistic workplace scenarios. Understand SHRM leadership principles. Learn to identify most vs least effective responses.

Practice Exams

Simulate the environment to gauge readiness, build stamina, identify gaps, and practice time management.

Stay Consistent

Even 30 to 60 minutes a day adds up. Stay positive and track milestones.
