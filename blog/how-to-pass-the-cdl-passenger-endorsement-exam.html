How to Pass the CDL Passenger Endorsement Exam

Mar 10, 2026 · 8 min read

Introduction

Earning a passenger endorsement on your CDL is a strategic move for professionals who want to drive vehicles carrying 16 or more passengers, including buses, airport shuttles, and school transportation vehicles.

What Is the CDL Passenger Endorsement?

The P endorsement allows commercial drivers to legally operate vehicles that transport passengers. Drivers must pass a knowledge test covering safety, vehicle inspection, emergency procedures, and passenger management.

Understanding the Test

The test focuses on pre-trip inspection procedures, loading and unloading, emergency handling and evacuation, communication with passengers, and general vehicle operation. Most exams consist of 20 to 30 multiple-choice questions with an 80% minimum passing score.

Steps to Prepare

Download your state CDL manual with its passenger endorsement section. Focus on emergency exits, brake systems, and loading procedures. Take practice tests. For busy professionals, a structured prep course can be more efficient.

Tips for Test Day

Get a good night of sleep. Arrive early with all required documents. Read each question carefully. Use process of elimination. Stay calm and confident.

Why It Matters

Drivers with specialized endorsements are in high demand. The passenger endorsement opens doors to stable, well-paying jobs in both public and private sectors.
