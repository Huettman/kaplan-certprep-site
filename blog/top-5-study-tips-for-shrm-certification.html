Top 5 Study Tips for SHRM Certification

Dec 10, 2025 · 6 min read

Introduction

Preparing for the SHRM-CP or SHRM-SCP exam while balancing a full-time job is challenging. Your study methods must be efficient and effective.

1. Create a Realistic Study Schedule

Block out 1 to 2 hours per session. Break down the SHRM BASK into manageable sections. Set weekly goals. Short daily sessions beat weekend cramming.

2. Use Official Study Resources

Use official SHRM learning materials and quality third-party resources. Study the SHRM BASK thoroughly. Use practice exams to identify gaps.

3. Active Learning Techniques

Take notes in your own words. Use flashcards. Teach concepts to a colleague. Active engagement improves recall during the exam.

4. Join a Study Group

Peer support provides motivation, accountability, and different perspectives. Share resources and quiz each other.

5. Practice with Exam Simulations

Timed practice exams build stamina and reduce anxiety. Review answers and focus on areas where you scored lower.
