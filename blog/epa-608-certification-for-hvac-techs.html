EPA 608 Certification for HVAC Techs

Aug 15, 2025 · 7 min read

Introduction

EPA 608 Certification is crucial for HVAC professionals to legally handle refrigerants and advance in the industry.

What Is EPA 608?

Federal requirement under Section 608 of the Clean Air Act. Four types: Type I for small appliances, Type II for high-pressure, Type III for low-pressure, and Universal covering all three.

Why It Matters

Demonstrates industry-standard knowledge, increases job opportunities, ensures regulatory compliance, and enhances credibility.

Exam Details

Four sections with 25 questions each. Must score at least 72% per section. Core is mandatory. Key topics: refrigerant types, ozone depletion, leak detection, recovery procedures, and safety.

Set a consistent study schedule. Use mobile resources. Take practice tests. Join a study group. Enroll in a prep course.
