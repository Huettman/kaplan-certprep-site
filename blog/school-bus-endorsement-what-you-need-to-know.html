School Bus Endorsement What You Need to Know

Jul 5, 2025 · 6 min read

Introduction

For working professionals interested in expanding their commercial driving careers, earning a CDL school bus endorsement is a smart move. This specialized endorsement allows you to legally operate a school bus, opening up job opportunities in both public and private sectors.

What Is the S Endorsement?

An additional certification on your CDL that allows you to operate a school bus. Typically added to a Class B or Class C CDL. Required by law in most states for anyone transporting students.

Why Get It?

Job security as school bus drivers are always in demand. Flexible hours with part-time or split-shift schedules. Community impact through student safety. Supplemental income opportunity.

Requirements

Hold a valid CDL or be applying for one. Pass a background check including fingerprinting. Pass a written knowledge test specific to school bus operation. Complete a skills test in a school bus. Meet medical certification requirements. Some states require additional training hours.

Preparing for the Exam

Study the CDL manual school bus section covering emergency exits, railroad crossing procedures, and student behavior management. Take practice tests that simulate the real test environment. Enroll in an online prep course for structured learning and expert guidance.

Maintaining Your Endorsement

Most states require periodic renewal, which may include refresher training, updated background checks, and medical recertification. Staying current ensures continued eligibility.

Conclusion

A CDL school bus endorsement is a worthwhile investment for professionals seeking career flexibility, job security, and the opportunity to make a difference.
