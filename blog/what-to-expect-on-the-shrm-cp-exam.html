What to Expect on the SHRM CP Exam

Dec 20, 2025 · 7 min read

Introduction

The SHRM-CP exam is a pivotal step for HR professionals who want to validate their knowledge and abilities.

Exam Structure

The exam tests behavioral competencies and HR functional knowledge. It consists of 160 questions, 130 scored and 30 unscored, with a four-hour time limit.

Types of Questions

Knowledge-based questions test factual information. Situational judgment questions assess your ability to apply knowledge in real-world scenarios.

Content Areas

Behavioral Competencies make up 50% covering leadership, communication, consultation, and ethical practice. HR Knowledge Domains make up the other 50% covering people, organization, workplace, and strategy.

Scoring

Raw scores convert to a scaled score from 120 to 200. You need a minimum of 200 to pass. Results available in three to four weeks.

Preparing

Review the SHRM BASK framework. Take practice exams. Use official learning resources. Create a study schedule that fits your commitments.
