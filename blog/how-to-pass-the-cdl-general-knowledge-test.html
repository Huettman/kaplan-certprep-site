How to Pass the CDL General Knowledge Test

Aug 10, 2025 · 6 min read

Introduction

Earning a CDL is a strategic career move. Before you can get behind the wheel, you need to pass the CDL General Knowledge Test. Proper preparation is essential to success.

What Is the Test?

A written exam required for all commercial driver applicants. It assesses understanding of vehicle safety, inspection procedures, driving techniques, and transport regulations. Typically 50 multiple-choice questions with an 80% passing score required. The core topics are consistent nationwide.

Key Topics Covered

Vehicle inspection and maintenance. Basic vehicle control. Shifting gears. Speed management and space management. Hazard perception and emergency situations. Alcohol and drug regulations. Transporting cargo safely.

Prep Strategies for Working Professionals

Create a realistic study schedule with even 30 minutes daily making a difference. Use the official CDL Manual from your state DMV as your best source. Practice tests help you become familiar with question formats and identify weak areas. Study on the go with mobile apps, flashcards, and audio lessons. Join a study group or course for accountability and insights.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

Skipping the official CDL manual. Relying solely on outdated practice tests. Not managing test anxiety or rushing through questions. Underestimating the importance of pre-trip inspection topics.

Test Day Tips

Get a good night of sleep and arrive early. Bring necessary identification and documents. Take your time reading each question carefully. Eliminate obviously incorrect answers before choosing. Trust your training and stay calm.
