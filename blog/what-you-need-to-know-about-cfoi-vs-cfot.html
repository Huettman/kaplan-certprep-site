What You Need to Know About CFOI vs CFOT

Oct 10, 2025 · 7 min read

Introduction

CFOI and CFOT are the two most recognized fiber optic certifications. Each serves a different purpose and targets different expertise levels.

CFOT Certification

Offered by the Fiber Optic Association. Entry-level. Covers fiber optic theory, cable types, connectorization, splicing methods, and testing and troubleshooting.

CFOI Certification

Offered by ETA International. For experienced professionals. Covers installation procedures, industry codes, detailed splicing and termination, and advanced testing. Requires two years of verified experience.

Key Differences

CFOT is entry-level, CFOI for experienced professionals. Different organizations. CFOT covers core concepts, CFOI covers advanced practices. CFOT is a stepping stone, CFOI opens supervisory roles.

Which Is Right?

Entering the field: CFOT. Have experience: CFOI.

Final Thoughts

Both build credibility and demonstrate commitment to excellence in fiber optics.
