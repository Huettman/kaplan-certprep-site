CDL Endorsements Which Ones Should You Get

Jun 10, 2025 · 7 min read

Introduction

If you are working toward your CDL, choosing the right endorsements is essential for aligning your CDL with your career goals. This guide helps you understand which endorsements are available and which are worth pursuing.

What Is a CDL Endorsement?

Additional qualifications on your commercial driver's license required to transport certain cargo or operate specialized vehicles. You must pass additional written tests and sometimes skills tests administered by your state DMV.

Top Endorsements to Consider

H Endorsement for Hazardous Materials: required for transporting flammable liquids, explosives, or toxic substances. High demand and higher pay. Requires TSA background check. N Endorsement for Tank Vehicles: for liquid or gaseous materials. Often paired with HazMat. X Endorsement combining Tanker and HazMat: ideal for chemical transport and fuel delivery. T Endorsement for Double/Triple Trailers: commonly used in long-haul freight. P Endorsement for Passenger Vehicles: for buses, shuttles, and transit. S Endorsement for School Bus: requires additional background checks.

How to Choose

Research job listings to see which endorsements employers require. Talk to industry professionals about the best opportunities. Consider long-term goals as obtaining endorsements early can set you up for future success.

Prepare with Confidence

Passing the endorsement exams requires focused preparation and solid understanding of specialized driving rules and regulations. The payoff in job flexibility and salary potential is well worth the effort.
